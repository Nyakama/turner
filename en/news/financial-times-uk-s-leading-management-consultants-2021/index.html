Financial Times UK’s leading management consultants 2021

18 February 2021

We have been featured for the second successive year in the Financial Times ranking of the UK’s Leading Management Consultants 2021, in the Construction and Infrastructure category.

The annual rating is based on recommendations by clients and peers. These are collected through surveys conducted by Statista, an independent data company. A total of 213 consultancies are listed this year.

Patricia Moore, Managing Director UK, said:

2021 marks our 75th anniversary and in that time we've grown into one of the world’s largest professional services providers to the construction and infrastructure sector.

"Doing so has only been possible because of the confidence that our clients have in our ability to help them successfully deliver their projects and programmes.

"We face the urgent need to drive the post-COVID recovery, rebalance the economy and tackle the climate emergency, all this while our industry undergoes a dramatic transformation. In this context, forming trusted partnerships is more important than ever and it is the only way to assure the right outcomes for our clients every time.”
