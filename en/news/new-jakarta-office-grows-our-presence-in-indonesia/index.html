New Jakarta office grows our presence in Indonesia

26 April 2023

We have relocated our office in Jakarta to support our expansion plans in Indonesia.

Our presence in Indonesia has expanded rapidly since we entered the market in 2013. With a focus on high-tech manufacturing and data centres, our Jakarta-based team has grown from 20 to nearly 200 people in the last five years.

The relocation to a new, larger office on Level 37 of International Financial Centre Tower 2, is part of our strategic commitment to provide a broader range of professional services to both local and global clients in country. It also expands our offering in the Indonesian market to include corporate offices, mixed-used developments, hotels, malls and large-scale commercial and infrastructure projects.

Our enhanced professional services include net-zero and digitalisation solutions tailored to fit specific client needs, in addition to our programme, project and cost-management expertise.

Sumit Mukherjee, Regional Director, Indonesia & Philippines, said:

I am confident that our new, modern and collaborative Jakarta office will provide the ideal environment for our expanding Indonesian team to strengthen our project- delivery expertise across industries and enhance our support of our existing and new clients.

"With access to the latest tools for digitalisation and sustainability strategies, we are well-positioned to be a hub for project management and delivery in cities across Indonesia and the rest of Southeast Asia."
