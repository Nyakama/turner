Kenya wins at the Real Estate Excellence Awards 2020

18 December 2020

We have been named the Best Cost Management Consultancy at the 2020 Kenya Real Estate Excellence awards, held virtually on Friday 18 December.

The Real Estate Excellence Awards recognise and celebrate the key players across the real estate sector who have achieved outstanding results and delivered positive outcomes for their clients.

This is our third win at the awards; in 2018 and 2019 we won Best Construction and Project Management Consultant. Now being awarded Best Cost Management Consultancy recognises our continued success in the East African construction industry and our ongoing commitment to service excellence and client satisfaction. Despite the volatility in the industry this year, our business has remained strong.

John Rogers, our Regional Director for East Africa commented on the win:

Being named Best Cost Management Consultancy of the Year is an important milestone for us as it is testimony to our commitment to our clients and their projects across the industry.

"2020 has been a volatile year for most businesses across the globe, and in East Africa our relationships with clients have grown stronger than ever. I am very proud of the team’s achievements."

Cost Management Director Rewel Kariuki added:

This award is a recognition of our commitment to delivering great outcomes for our clients so that they experience a consistently high level of service delivery and relationship management across the region.

"As part of our cost management offering, we use industry leading tools, market insight and high-quality data to provide our clients with greater cost confidence for their capital projects.”
