Ryan Shalabi wins 'Construction Executive of the Year' award

07 September 2020

Ryan Shalabi, Oman Country Manager and Head of Contract Services for the Middle East received the accolade of 'Construction Executive of the Year' at the Construction Week Oman Virtual Awards 2020 on 2 September.

Ryan was commended for leading the 184 percent growth of our Oman office, as well as delivering major projects in the region. Combining his extensive knowledge of the Omani market with his transformational leadership style, he has stabilised the business and built a platform for sustainable growth.

Commenting on his win, Ryan Shalabi said:

I’m extremely honoured to be awarded Construction Executive of the Year at the Construction Week Oman Awards 2020. It goes without saying that this recognition would not have been attainable without the support of the excellent team I have behind me, who tirelessly focus on making a difference in the Sultanate of Oman.

Ryan is passionate about tackling some of the region’s biggest challenges to the successful completion of construction projects, including dispute resolution. He proactively raises awareness of how to keep contracts moving through effective dispute avoidance and sits on a number of regional panels. With over 20 years’ experience in the field, he is also a recommended construction expert witness with Who’s Who Legal.

Now in its ninth year, the Construction Week Oman awards have become a firm fixture in the social calendar for the construction community in the Sultanate of Oman. Designed to recognise and reward individual excellence, corporate prowess, project success and sustainable efforts within 14 categories, the awards provide a platform for companies to present their best work.

One of the most prestigious award categories, ‘Construction Executive of the Year’ is given to the individual who has demonstrated the greatest impact on their construction-related business in the past 12 months.

Find out more about the awards and watch the virtual ceremony.
