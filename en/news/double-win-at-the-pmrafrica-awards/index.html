Double win at the PMR.africa Awards

01 April 2022

Our South Africa team has picked up a Diamond Arrow and Golden Arrow award at the PMR.africa Awards ceremony that took place at the Royal Johannesburg Golf Club on 31 March 2022.

Acknowledging our quantity surveying and project management expertise, this is the fourth year consecutively that we have won the awards – a true testament to our commitment to clients and delivering exceptional quality.

PMR.africa is a leading South African market research company which celebrates excellence across industry sectors. The organisation’s mission is to set a benchmark for African businesses to aspire to, and to recognise outstanding performance.

Key to PMR.africa’s work is conducting an annual survey that involves interviewing hundreds of professionals from the African construction sector, including architects, construction companies, consulting engineers and property developers.

Businesses are surveyed across a range of performance indicators, including their capacity for innovation, Black Economic Empowerment, cost control, client liaison and ethical business practices.

Stephen McCartney, Managing Director, Africa, commented:

It is an honour to be named the best quantity surveying firm in South Africa for the fourth year in a row by PMR.africa. Four years ago, we set a target to become the market leader in the provision of cost management and project management services across South Africa. We are pleased that our efforts are being recognised, but humble enough to realise that we have to continue to do better every day and attract and retain talent to sustain this ambition.
