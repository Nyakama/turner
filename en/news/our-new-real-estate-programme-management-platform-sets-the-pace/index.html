Our new real estate programme management platform sets the pace>

25 February 2020

We have launched our new digital programme management platform, pace>, which helps real estate owners and developers to drive efficiencies in the delivery of their capital programmes.

Clients often find information management on complex programmes challenging and time consuming. pace> offers a solution.

Our integrated platform automates project and programme management processes. It allows for faster, more streamlined delivery while maintaining control and providing consistent, reliable information.

The platform also integrates seamlessly with a range of proprietary software solutions to offer a comprehensive management solution.

Daniel Granger, Head of Real Estate Programme Management said:

We have developed the platform in partnership with CAMMS, combining their leading software with our depth of expertise in real estate programme management.

By bringing together real-time project and programme data analytics on a simple dashboard, the platform gives our clients full visibility of data, which speeds up their decision making and improves overall performance.
