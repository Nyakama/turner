Appointed on world-class Sydney Metro project

05 March 2020

We're proud to be providing Project Management Office (PMO) services on the once-in-a-century Sydney Metro West project. The PMO contract follows a successful working partnership with the New South Wales (NSW) government’s Sydney Metro authority on the now operational Sydney Metro Northwest line.

Sydney Metro West brings a world-class metro rail line to the city’s west. It is the largest part of the multi-billion Sydney Metro network, which is Australia’s largest public transport project.

The project is planning to deliver about 24 kilometres of twin underground tunnels between Westmead and the Sydney CBD, linking new communities to rail services which will service 3.2 million people living in Western Sydney by 2036.

Darren Munton, Head of Infrastructure, Australia and New Zealand said:

“We were incredibly proud to play a significant role in the success of Sydney Metro Northwest’s opening in 2019, delivered $1 billion under budget. Our relationship with Sydney Metro and Transport for NSW extends over a decade and is testament to a collaborative working environment focused on high quality outcomes and exceptional standards of service.

“The team has developed an enviable track record in providing integrated services to support complex local and global transport projects. World’s best practice project management principles and lessons learnt from Sydney Metro’s Northwest and the under-construction City & Southwest lines will be applied to Sydney Metro West.”

Sydney Metro West will connect Greater Parramatta with the Sydney CBD and will double the rail capacity of this current rail corridor with a travel time target between the two centres of about 20 minutes.

Anooj Oodit, Managing Director, Australia and New Zealand comments:

“Over the past decade the team has worked very hard to solidify our reputation as one of the leading providers of cost and project management services working on major infrastructure projects. We currently work on most of Australia’s major transport infrastructure projects.

“Sydney Metro West’s infrastructure investment will bring considerable social and economic benefits by cutting travel times and reducing congestion for commuters to and from major employment hubs. We look forward to playing an important role in creating a legacy transport project that reinforces Sydney’s position as a thriving global destination.”

Our PMO services include capital cost planning, risk management, schedule management and operating and maintenance cost planning.

The formal planning process has now begun, including confirmation of new metro station locations at Westmead, Parramatta, Sydney Olympic Park, North Strathfield, Burwood North, Five Dock and The Bays Precinct.
