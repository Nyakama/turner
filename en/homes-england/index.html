About the Framework

We have been appointed to the Homes England Multidisciplinary and Technical Services Framework: 2019 â€“ 2023, to help Homes England deliver its strategic plan to accelerate housing delivery in areas of greatest demand.

The team provides the full range of Homes England Multi-Disciplinary services to support projects at any stage of the development process. From early feasibility and due diligence, through master-planning and design, supporting Homes England funding bids and appraisals, securing the necessary third-party and town planning consents, and in planning and running engagement with external stakeholders and communities.

What is Homes England?

Homes England is the non-departmental public body that funds new affordable housing in England. It releases land for developers to build new homes, improve current neighbourhoods and grow communities. Its aims are to increase the number of new homes that are built, including affordable homes; improve existing affordable homes; increase the supply of public land and speeding up the rate at which it can be built on and intervening in the market to stimulate growth.

Who can use the framework

The Multidisciplinary Panel is available for use free of charge by other public sector bodies.

The framework has been procured through the fully compliant Official Journal of the European Union (OJEU) processes, and offers a quick and efficient means to procure high quality, good value development related technical services including:

  • Project and Cost Management
  • Development Monitoring
  • Corporate Risk Management and Governance
  • Technical and Site Investigation
  • Engineering Design
  • Architecture and Landscape Design
  • Planning
  • Consultation
  • Masterplanning and Urban Design
  • Construction Design & Management (CDM) Services
  • Property Advisory Services.
