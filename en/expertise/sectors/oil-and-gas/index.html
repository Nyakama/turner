Driving decarbonisation and capital efficiency

With global demand for oil remaining high and natural gas accounting for around a quarter of global electricity generation, traditional energy remains central to supporting and improving energy access for all.

Alongside delivery of sustainable energy supply, a commercially successful sector is delivering hydrocarbons with the lowest possible emissions and enabling the transition to cleaner energy technologies to be accelerated. Decarbonising existing assets is a priority, with pathways including such options as reducing flaring and emissions, carbon capture, utilisation and storage to sequester carbon dioxide safely and electrification using renewable technologies.

The sector faces many challenges in delivery and capital efficiency continues to be the key consideration for investment, upgrade and expansion and maintenance and turnaround projects. The drive for value extends across all upstream, midstream and downstream facilities.

Our work with clients extends across the entire value chain of the oil and gas industry, where we are driving decarbonisation through such measures as carbon capture and deployment of renewables and green hydrogen.
