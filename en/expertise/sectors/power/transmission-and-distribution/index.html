Equipping transmission and distribution for the future

Pressure on transmission and distribution infrastructure is increasing as global operators look to serve their growing markets, driving a focus on enhancing efficiency in energy delivery across the sector. At a network level, smart grids are deploying advanced technology to help reduce waste and cost. For local distribution, quick and efficient connections are requiring more responsive supply chains.

Operators are prioritising environmental targets to drive the transition to a greener, more sustainable energy sector. Security is also a concern, with systems facing cyber attacks and substations, transformers and power lines vulnerable to physical threats.

Whether for new build or for existing assets incorporating new technology configurations, conventional power and renewable energy programmes comprise interconnected projects. These diverse projects can include plant construction, technology integration and storage and grid infrastructure as part of the transition to more sustainable energy.

Our expertise in transmission and distribution

Whether supporting the delivery of systems in remote locations or helping to drive a transition to renewables, we bring expertise to programmes and projects.

Our transmission expertise includes high voltage overhead transmission, underground transmission systems including subsea, high voltage and medium voltage substations, HVDC as well as medium-voltage distribution systems, and protection and control systems.

We bring global knowledge and business intelligence to inform clients’ decision-making. That is supported by consultants who understand local regulation, environmental constraints and supply chain management.

Transmission and distribution services

Our services span the full lifecycle of programmes and projects supporting the reliable distribution of power. Our programme and project management expertise, cost and commercial management, project controls, procurement and advisory services help clients meet the sector’s challenges.

Our industry knowledge enables us to drive value and performance through robust cost control, while managing and mitigating risk. Across programmes and projects, our services are dedicated to enabling lifetime value and success.

Advisory

We connect clients and their programmes to market innovation and global leading practice, supporting opportunities for successful infrastructure investment and benchmarking performance to aid decision-making.

Programme strategy and set up

We work with our clients and key stakeholders to establish the business case and build the team capabilities and execution plan to drive successful delivery.

Programme management

We have a reputation for devising the strategy, setting up and managing programme management office and multi-disciplinary programme teams.

Project management

Our independent advice and expertise enables us to negotiate the complexities and risk, apply learning, realise opportunities and deliver on clients’ cost, schedule and performance targets.

Cost and commercial management

We optimise cost performance at every stage, balancing clients’ market needs with robust cost control and applying our methodologies, cost models, data and market intelligence to drive cost-efficiency and value.
