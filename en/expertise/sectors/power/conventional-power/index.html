Meeting demand with conventional power

A transition to low carbon energy is under way, but traditional oil, coal and gas remain important to global power supply, particularly for developing nations. Owners and operators in the conventional power sector are under pressure to keep pace with market demand and ensure reliability and security of supply by maintaining or improving output and efficiency. This must be balanced with effective cost control in production and the reduction of environmental impacts.

Investors, owners and operators are evolving their business strategies to drive output and efficiency while enabling a more sustainable, low carbon future. As they look to the latter, organisations are increasingly considering divesting and diversifying their plants and decarbonising production processes. This can present new opportunities for the sector.

Our expertise in conventional power

We bring global reach and local knowledge to programmes and projects in the conventional power sector. Over the past 30 years we have worked on more than US$700Bn of conventional power projects.

Our support spans project stages from initial investment appraisal to delivery and ongoing asset management. Our benchmarking data, knowledge and best practice learning drive solutions that promote cost-efficiency, performance and value. They bring certainty in project cost, schedule and risk.

Through a whole-life perspective, we help to maximise return on investment. Our guidance extends to strategies for decommissioning aged facilities.

Conventional power services

We support clients with our expertise in project and programme management, cost, contract and commercial management, project controls and advisory and procurement. Our services are dedicated to helping clients overcome the challenges of the power sector and maximise project and programme potential.

Our independent, evidence-based advice drives effective evaluation of project feasibility, scope, risk, execution and constructability. We support the programme lifecycle, laying the foundations for success and driving performance.
