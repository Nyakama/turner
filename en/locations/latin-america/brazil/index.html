Turner & Townsend in Brazil

Our expertise is helping our clients operate more effectively in a challenging construction market.

While the construction industry in Brazil is facing challenging times, there is great potential for future sustainable growth and prosperity. After drastic cuts in public investment following years of overspend, steps are being taken to boost low confidence among investors.

Making the difference in Brazil

Known as Turner & Townsend Consultoria Limitada in Brazil, we have been operating in the country for over ten years. Headquartered in São Paulo since 2010, we also have operations in Rio de Janeiro, Belo Horizonte and many other Brazilian states.

Our focus is on providing a full range of quantity surveying, cost management, project management, management consulting, contract services, claim analysis and project controls in the real estate, infrastructure and natural resources sectors. We are currently managing more than USD1bn of projects in the country.

Luiz Buff

Country Manager

Luiz has over 20 years’ experience in a variety of sectors in real estate and infrastructure.
