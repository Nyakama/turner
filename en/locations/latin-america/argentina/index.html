Turner & Townsend in Argentina

Following a decade of economic headwinds, the Argentinian economy looks set to improve as reforms provide confidence that construction activity will increase.

The reforms aim at increasing construction investment, fewer legal obstacles, and the ending of lawsuits that prevented access to global capital.

The government is set to boost infrastructure spending across many sectors including airports, freight rail, roads, renewable energy, mining, tunnels and power plants.

Making the difference in Argentina

Headquartered in Buenos Aires our team of experts work across our three operating sectors to support private and public sector clients. Our work includes contract administration, project controls, project management, cost management and consultancy in PPP.
