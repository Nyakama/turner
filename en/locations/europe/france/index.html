Turner & Townsend in France

Select a language:
  • English
  • français

France is proving to be highly attractive to international businesses and investors. We’re helping clients achieve the best return on their investment in this complex and mature market.

The real estate sector is benefiting from a healthy level of renovations and new developments. In Paris especially there is considerable activity around the upgrade of retail outlets, the renewal of office spaces and the development of high-end hotels. At the same time, major infrastructure and development projects continue to inject dynamism into the engineering and construction sectors.

Making the difference in France

Turner & Townsend has been operating in France since 1995. From our new Paris office, we deliver our core services of programme management, project management and cost management. Our main focus is to closely support international clients across multiple sectors.

France is a mature market which international clients can find difficult to operate in. Our teams understand the local context and ensure we deliver the great outcomes Turner & Townsend are known for.

Our presence allows us to rapidly deploy on site when required. We also have a thorough understanding of the local business and regulatory environment, and are used to overcoming the challenges raised by different languages, cultures and project practices.
