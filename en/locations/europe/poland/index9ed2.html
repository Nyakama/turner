Turner & Townsend in Poland

Select a language:
  • English
  • polski

Poland has shown strong resilience throughout the pandemic avoiding some of the economic challenges present in other European locations. However, despite a strong growth prognosis, the country is currently facing high inflation and a rapid increase in construction labour and material prices.

Having a thorough understanding of these issues, we are well-placed to advise international and local investors, helping them to maximise opportunities and minimise risks.

We see growing international demand for our services in the data centre and industrial and logistics sectors, where we help our clients navigate local market policy and procedures, leveraging our expertise and incorporating innovative solutions.

With sustainability and net zero becoming more important in the market, we have supported a local real estate developer in delivering one of the most sustainable office buildings in Europe – V.Offices.

As Poland is increasingly seen as a location for, not only outsourcing centres, but also a highly skilled workforce, we are working with the world’s leading clients to deliver their offices across Poland.

Making the difference in Poland

Working in the market for the past 10 years from two offices in Krakow and Warsaw, we support local and international clients across Poland including AFI Europe, Zurich Insurance, Vantage DC, Shell, Helical, Tesco Nissan and many more.

Our team of over 45 experienced professionals leverage their local market expertise and proactive approach to add value to a variety of projects in corporate and office, data centres, industrial and logistics, retail and hotels and hospitality.

We’ve embedded innovative digital solutions across our service offering to transform performance for our clients and reduce risk We distinguish ourselves by delivering high-quality services to our clients and focus on finding the best solutions on a project-by-project basis:

  • We are recognised as leaders in identifying savings for our clients through our cost management services. In Krakow, we have established a Cost Management Centre of Excellence – a team of highly qualified and experienced cost engineers. Applying innovative technology and expertise they are adding value to complex projects.
  • Based on our proven project and programme management approach, combined with our local Polish market knowledge and use of innovative technology, we actively mitigate risks and transform performance.
