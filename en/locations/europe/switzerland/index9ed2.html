In today's complex and constantly changing world, our clients want to see results that make a difference, from the conception of a project to its completion. We listen to and understand your requirements, supporting you to deliver the results you need with our expertise.

We offer services that cover the entire spectrum of the project life cycle, from strategic advisory during business case definition, feasibility studies and project set-up, to management of the procurement, design and construction phases, through to project completion.

With the expertise of our international network and local market knowledge, we offer a risk averse and proactive approach to address challenges and opportunities to secure successful delivery of your project or programme.

Learn more about who we are.

Making the difference in Switzerland

We have been working for the Swiss pharmaceutical industry for many years, which led to the opening of our first office in Basel in the mid-2000s. Presence in the local market and close proximity to our clients is instrumental to the delivery of the high standards for which we are known internationally. Our Swiss offices now support local and international clients across Switzerland, including Zurich Insurance, Credit Suisse, Lonza, Johnson & Johnson and Microsoft.

The growing volume of projects for our clients led to the opening of our second office in Zurich. With the Zurich office, we have supplemented our Basel office life science expertise with strong capability in the commercial real estate sector. In addition to our two locations in Basel and Zurich, we are also represented by a branch in Geneva, where we provide services for the pharmaceutical industry as well as for the real estate sector.

Our team is comprised of project and cost managers with a professional background in architecture, engineering, quantity surveyor and construction management. In addition to technical expertise, we also have the linguistic skills to support our clientsâ€™ projects in German, French, Italian and English.

We focus on the core professional consultancy services of projects to define our clientsâ€™ requirements, tailoring proven processes to your needs and optimising their implementation for the successful delivery of your projects.

The core capabilities of our team include project management, programme management, project controls & performance, and cost and commercial management.

We support our Swiss clients in the definition, planning and implementation of their projects through all phases of the local Swiss Society of Engineers and Architects (SIA) delivery structure. The projects we manage range from new build, conversion and refurbishment, to complex tenant fit-out.
