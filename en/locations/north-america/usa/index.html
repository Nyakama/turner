Turner & Townsend in USA

We’re helping clients achieve their business goals in one of the world’s largest construction markets.

The USA has abundant natural resources, is the largest producer of oil and natural gas, and is the world’s second-largest manufacturer. Of the 500 largest companies in the world, 128 are headquartered here.

As pressures mount from a growing population, global competition and a need to modernise ageing infrastructure, steady market growth will continue. With this growth comes re-framed priorities around investments, spatial diversity, architecture, technology, sustainability and social impact.

Making the difference in the USA

Our U.S. team comprises more than 700 employees, working across 19 offices. Within the region, we specialise in project and cost management, risk, contracts and procurement, lean construction practices, and life cycle asset management.

Over the past 20 years, we have provided consultancy services on the largest and most iconic projects in the United States, including major metro transit authority programmes and corporate campuses.

Across private and public sectors, our talented professionals provide businesses with the skills and insight to improve the way they develop, deliver and operate their physical real estate assets. Leveraging our global footprint and our collective expertise, our team provides the most innovative solutions for our clients’ greatest challenges.

CBRE Heery transitioned into our business from CBRE, Inc. in January 2023. The transfer is a critical step in aligning our joint service offerings for clients and establishing CBRE and Turner & Townsend as the premier programme, project and cost management provider in the U.S.

As a combined team, CBRE and Turner & Townsend have the capabilities and the footprint to ensure superior services for any project requirement of any size or complexity. CBRE Heery is now a wholly owned subsidiary of Turner & Townsend Inc. and it will continue to operate under the legal entity name “CBRE Heery”.

Our services

We have developed a range of expertise with the breadth and depth to cover every area of project planning, delivery and business improvement, at every stage of the project or business life cycle. It means that the solutions we develop for you are based on a deep understanding of your overall objectives, and it gives us the perspective to recognise the opportunities that will generate a greater advantage for you.
