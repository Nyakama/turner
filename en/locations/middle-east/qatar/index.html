Turner & Townsend in Qatar

The construction sector in Qatar is booming, with a raft of spectacular large-scale mega-projects set to roll out over the next few years. We’re helping clients thrive in this dynamic environment.

The 2022 FIFA World Cup has been a significant growth driver for Qatar, with plans underway for eight new multi-billion dollar stadia and the wider market benefitting from scaled-up investments in infrastructure, tourism projects and commercial spaces. At the same time, higher labour costs, rising inflation and pressure to meet ambitious project deadlines have potential to disrupt expansion plans.

Making the difference in Qatar

Projects of such size and ambition demand a high level of expertise from teams involved. Our project teams are equipped with niche expertise, and each member is selected for their unique combination of specialist knowledge and sector experience, ensuring they are the best possible match for our clients’ specific requirements.

With over 90 employees based at our office in Doha, grown from just five in 2009, we support clients across the real estate, infrastructure and natural resources sectors.
