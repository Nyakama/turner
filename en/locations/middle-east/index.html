Turner & Townsend in the Middle East

Turner & Townsend has been supporting clients in the Middle East for more than thirty years. From a growing network of offices in the region, we are undertaking projects with some of the most progressive and successful organisations in the world, providing our clients with an essential combination of local knowledge and global best practice. We make the difference to our clients' success in every industry, from commercial, sports, leisure and hospitality to power, mining and metals and transport. From four regional offices we have worked in nineteen countries in the region.
