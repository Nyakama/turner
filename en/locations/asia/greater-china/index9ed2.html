Turner & Townsend in Greater China

Select a language:
  • English
  • 中文(简体)

We’re helping clients meet the diverse challenges emerging from the country’s transition to a consumer-driven economy.

As one of the main drivers of construction worldwide, China’s shift away from manufacturing and construction and towards services is having a far-reaching impact across sectors. Demand is high for innovative technologies and management tools that maximise energy saving and labour productivity and can keep up with the evolving needs of projects in a changing economy.

Making the difference in China

With 220 employees working from six offices our teams are a mix of local and global experts and combine local know-how with extensive global best practice experience.

We support a diverse range of clients and have recently completed multiple large-scale, high-profile projects in the retail, commercial and hospitality sectors.
