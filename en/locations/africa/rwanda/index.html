Turner & Townsend in Rwanda

Rwanda is fast becoming an attractive destination for businesses and investors. Our clients are trusting us to drive better business outcomes for their projects across all sectors.

Known as the land of a thousand hills, Rwanda is a landlocked country in the Great Rift Valley of East Africa. Its capital city is Kigali which is the hub of government and commercial activity.

Rwanda is ranked by the World Bank as one of the easiest countries to do business within Africa. The country is strategically positioning itself as the destination of choice in the region for education, innovation and tourism; as a result, industrial and logistics, corporate and office, residential and hotel sectors are growing to meet the demand.

Rwanda has a population of approximately 13 million people, 17.5 percent of whom live in urban areas. Greater urbanisation is key to Rwanda’s pursuit to achieve middle income country status. This accelerated urbanisation coupled with strong economic growth and demographic changes has also led to rapid growth in real estate.

Making the difference in Rwanda

Having operated in Rwanda for many years, we opened our Kigali office in 2020 to further support and get closer to our clients.

The team on the ground has significant experience in real estate and a deep understanding of the requirements to successfully deliver projects in Rwanda. Beyond traditional quantity surveying and cost management services, we offer major programme set-up, project management, project controls, assurance, project risk management, procurement and contract services and advisory services.

Our work includes that of many well-known clients and projects, including:

  • The Ellen DeGeneres Campus of the Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund
  • Kigali International Finance and Business Square
  • The One & Only Portfolio of Resorts and Lodges including Gorillas Nest and Nyungwe House
  • International School of Kigali
  • Coventry University Hub Rwanda
  • University of Global Health Equity
  • New Bugesera International Airport
  • Nobelia High Performance Tower
  • Trade Development Bank
  • Kigali Convention Centre (Claims Management Support)
  • Kigali Marriott Hotel
