Turner & Townsend in Africa

Turner & Townsend has been making the difference to our client's success in Africa for more than 30 years. We provide an essential combination of local knowledge and global best practice in every industry from mining and metals, power, utilities, commercial, sport, leisure and hospitality to public sector, health, education, oil and gas and transport. From nine offices we have worked in 34 countries in the region.
