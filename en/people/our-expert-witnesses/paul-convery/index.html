Paul Convery

BSc, LLM, MCIOB, MCIArb Director, Forensic Planning & Delay, UK

Highlights

  • Advising on major projects and claims exceeding £500m 
  • Infrastructure, natural resources and real estate sector experience 
  • Cross examination experience
  • Visually representing complex scenarios 
  • 15 years of experience 

Paul is a Director of Contract Services, based in the UK. He regularly acts as an independent expert in delay and forensic planning, specialising in dispute avoidance and resolution. 

Paul has worked extensively in providing independent opinion in highly contentious environments, primarily in relation to matters associated with delay and disruption. Paul regularly provides independent support to live projects during construction with a focus on delivery, as well as participating in formal dispute resolution forums.  

With notable experience in leading forensic analysis in infrastructure (highways, rail, tunnelling, water), real estate (residential, commercial and education) and natural resources (offshore, wind and process), Paul has a broad knowledge base to call upon.  

In addition to detailed working knowledge of all mainstream forensic methodologies, one of Paul’s key attributes is the ability to distil highly technical matters and convey the issues concisely. This approach facilitates focused discussion between the parties on the key principles in dispute. 

We could not have received a better service from Paul. His output was of an extremely high standard which was all underpinned with both industry experience and a robust and solid contractual basis.

- Commercial Manager, infrastructure client
