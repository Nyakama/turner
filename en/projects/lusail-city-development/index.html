Lusail City Development, Doha

Explore
  1. Home
  2. Projects
  3. Lusail City Development, Doha

Supporting one of the world’s most visionary single developments

Lusail City is a brand new development 15 kilometers north of Doha, covering an area of 38 km2 subdivided into 19 different districts. 

Key information

Location:
Qatar
Date started:
2010
Completion date:
Ongoing
Client type:
Real estate development
Main services:
Cost management

The residential components of the development will offer 50,000 dwelling units in a mix of town houses, villas, and apartments. Other districts include retail, commercial (including iconic high rise towers), entertainment and hospitality, marine, medical, education , golf districts, and civic facilities.

The infrastructure services at Lusail City are designed to be state-of-the-art utilities serving over 560,000 residents and visitors.

  • 1200 MVA of power provided by three grid primary substations
  • 80m liters potable water per day
  • 150km Of roads, including six exterior interchanges and over 100 interior signalised junctions
  • 21.2km Of light rail, 33 stations including eight underground

Our contribution

We have been commissioned to provide pre and post contract cost management services on a four year appointment. We provide industry experts and best practice to manage the construction and consultancy work with regard to cost and entitlement. The client has extended this commission for a further four years.

Making the difference

We have introduced and developed a world class, comprehensive cost management online tool that gives the client a total and accurate picture of the costs of this massive development. We have integrated our services with the client’s organisation to provide a single point of reporting and a shared responsibility to successfully deliver this project. We have produced a gap analysis of the client’s original procedures and developed a new suite of cost management procedures drawing from our global experience delivering similar work.
