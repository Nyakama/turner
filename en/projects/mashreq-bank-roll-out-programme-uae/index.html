Mashreq Bank roll-out programme, UAE

Explore

  3. Mashreq Bank roll-out programme, UAE

Enhancing customers' banking experience through better space design.

We were brought in for project management and to provide on-site support.

Key information

Location:
Mashreq Bank
Date started:
2018
Completion date:
Ongoing
Client type:
Retail
Main services:
Project management

Client’s requirements

Mashreq Bank has embarked on a roll-out programme aimed at delivering enhanced customer experience.

The revamp of Mashreq’s retail banking branches includes refreshed and contemporary interior design concept. The new look is driven by a desire to offer an efficient, creative and bespoke environment at Mashreq’s branches across the UAE. 

Our contribution

Turner & Townsend has been commissioned to provide project management support. This includes work and close collaboration with internal and external stakeholders in order to manage the overall programme and provide a strategy for a successful programme delivery.

The role also includes provision of support to the in house delivery team.

Making the difference

Having successfully delivered numerous retail roll-out programmes across the globe, Turner & Townsend understands the importance of brand and on time delivery thus bringing best industry practices to the commission.

Furthermore, Turner & Townsend has deployed highly specialised individuals with large scale regional retail experience to support the delivery of Mashreq’s programme.
