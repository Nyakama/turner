The Trees, India

Explore
  1. Home
  2. Projects
  3. The Trees, India

A sustainable mixed-use development in Mumbai’s greenest district.

The Trees, Godrej Properties’ flagship project is one of India's most sustainably planned mixed-use developments. The award-winning master plan set across 34 acres has residential, grade A office, hotel and retail spaces integrated within a walkable landscape programmed with amenities.

Key information

Location:
Mumbai, India
Date started:
2016
Completion date:
2021
Client type:
Real estate
Main services:
Cost and commercial management for the Residences 1 & 2.

Sustainable development

All assets within The Trees are IGBC Platinum-rated under relevant rating systems. The residential precinct consists of a perimeter block and towers around a central courtyard.

The clubhouse building scales down the towers to provide a distinctive resident experience with a gym, spa, squash court, multipurpose hall, mini theatre, restaurant and swimming pool.

Trusted relationship

We have worked with Godrej for over a decade and were brought on board with the mandate to provide value engineering advice and share local market trends.

We reported on cash flows, contractor work valuation and monthly budget preparation, and assisted in stakeholder communication and billing processes to enable timely project delivery.

Satish Jadhav, Head of Projects (Vikhroli Zone) at Godrej Properties Ltd, says:

We have had a good collaborative engagement with Turner & Townsend on all three of our residential phases at The Trees. Their team worked closely with us through all project stages and provided consistent support to optimise cost efficiencies and help bring our vision for this development to life.
