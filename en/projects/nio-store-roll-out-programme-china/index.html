NIO store roll-out programme, China

Explore

  3. NIO store roll-out programme, China

Bringing next-generation green vehicles to China’s consumers

Global smart electric vehicle company, NIO offers users a premium service as it seeks to push technological boundaries, while striving for a more sustainable world. In June 2022, the company delivered 12,961 vehicles - a new monthly record.

Key information

Location:
China
Date started:
2022
Completion date:
2023
Client type:
real estate
Main services:
Project management

Expansion plans

NIO wants to expand its businesses globally and this year is continuing to develop retail and service networks across China, across three store formats, NIO House, NIO Service Centre, and NIO Space.

Besides the basic car showroom functions, NIO is committed to creating a user community that grows together, offering its NIO House concept as a unique living space that connects people with the city.

Ultimately the company wants to gain market share in the highly competitive Chinese automotive market and to provide high-quality services to its clients

Bringing experience to bear

Our rich automotive delivery experience and successes over the past 25 years in China means that we can provide comprehensive project management services to NIO as it undertakes its expansion.

We are supporting the company on site, keeping the project on schedule while managing the quality control, and using our PMO experience to oversee the programme governance.

By integrating our programme management team, we are able to deliver this multi-faceted project with consistent quality standards, on time and on budget and in line with NIO’s goals.
