On-demand recording: How digital is transforming the role of the project manager

05 June 2020

Digital technology has fundamentally changed people’s lives and impacted on the way businesses operate.

To keep pace with market demands, the construction industry is innovating in its use of digital technology with moves aimed at improving ways of working with clients and project teams and delivering smarter and more sustainable buildings. But what does this mean for the future of managing projects and the people responsible?

During this 15 minute on-demand recording we are joined by Andy Norgett, Project Director at international property development company Ballymore.

Listen to discover:

  • If the project manager role, as we know it, will still exist in five years’ time
  • How digital helps improve collaboration and productivity on capital projects
  • The legacy benefits of integrating digital into asset delivery.

Our guest speaker

Andy Norgett, Project Director at Ballymore. With 18 years of experience, Andy has worked on mega projects internationally, originally starting as a computer aided designer. He has a passion for new technologies and has completed a Masters in building information modelling management.
