Procurement resilience in a post-COVID world

Romi Alboreto

Director, Contract services

UK

Email +44 (0)20 7544 4392
Romi Alboreto 05 November 2020

The need for innovative and risk assessed public procurement solutions has never been greater since the advent of COVID-19.

In an on-demand webinar with law firm Clyde & Co, we explore the need for commercial and procurement practitioners to future proof contracts and build streamlined, defensible procurement processes.

For public sector buyers and suppliers, who may be experiencing disruption in their supply chains or contracts as a result of COVID-19 coupled with the existing Brexit challenges, the webinar offers guidance on how to mitigate potential issues and build resilience through uncertain times.

Speakers on the webinar included Director Romi Alboreto, Development Manager Mark Adams and Clyde & Co Partner David Hansom.

Listen to discover:

  • Key aspects to consider when setting procurement strategies in these uncertain times
  • Practical advice in the delivery stage to minimise challenges
  • How to make changes to contracts lawfully
  • What lies ahead after the UK/EU Brexit transition period ends.
