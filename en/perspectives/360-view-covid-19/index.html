Adapting to a changing world

This issue of 360°view offers a series of perspectives on how we are adapting to the global COVID-19 pandemic and what learnings we can instil in projects and programmes across the real estate, infrastructure and natural resources sectors. Chief Operating Officer James Dand introduces the series.

The COVID-19 outbreak has had an impact on how we all live and work and is likely to have significant implications for the foreseeable future. As the world addresses the pandemic and its consequences, we are now taking on board the lessons that have emerged for our cities and for the construction industry.
