Great place to work

Our people are at the heart of our success and will be core to the next phase in our growth. We pride ourselves of the talent and expertise of our people, providing opportunities for them to learn and develop throughout their careers, so they can reach their potential. We promote a diverse and inclusive workplace, and strive to create a healthy and safe work environment that promotes our people’s wellbeing.

Strength in diversity

Our success stems from our people and a diversity of experience, perspective and understanding. We strive to represent the communities in which we live and work, not only to ensure we can best meet our clients’ needs but also to open up opportunities in our society.

Our regional diversity route maps are informed by a global set of principles that address inclusive leadership and progression, recruiting for diversity and creating an environment where all our people can succeed.

We recognise that achieving gender equality continues to be one of the most pressing challenges our industry is facing which is why we have set a target to reach a 40:60 gender split across our business by 2025.

Read more about our commitment to diversity and inclusion.

Opportunities to develop

We give our people the support, training and rewards they need to excel, recognising that people thrive in different environments and with different support.

This includes exceptional opportunities to work internationally and gain experience to deliver outstanding results for clients on cutting-edge projects worldwide.

We place emphasis on building future leaders with the ambition and skills needed to drive our industry forward.

  • 33% female employees
  • 7.9/10 overall employee engagement score
  • 8.6/10 average score when asking our global team if people from all backgrounds are accepted for who they are
  • 1.1% increase in the number of women in the business in the past year

Wellbeing at work

We target the highest standards of workplace health, safety and wellbeing, recognising the benefits for our people, business and society and have achieved accreditation to BS OHSAS 18001:2008, the best practice standard for safety management systems.

In addition, we facilitate an open dialogue with our people to ensure that we not only hear their voice but listen to them and work together to continue to improve our working environment.
