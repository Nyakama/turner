Appointed to support BBC’s portfolio

31 May 2023

We have been selected to support the British Broadcasting Corporation (BBC) on a range of projects across its large and varied property portfolio, which stretches across the whole of England, Northern Ireland, Scotland and Wales, including remote locations.

The four-year agreement, which has been awarded on the broadcaster’s construction consultancy services framework, is divided into four lots covering project management, architecture, engineering design and cost management.

Through this framework agreement we will provide construction project management (lot one), cost management and construction design management (CDM) and principal designer services (lot four). 

This award will build on our current work with BBC Workplace at Broadcasting House, helping them to transform the iconic building into a modern, sustainable workplace. 

Mark Ward, Director of UK Real Estate, said:

This appointment strengthens our long-standing relationship with the BBC and supports them on their journey to provide a modern, sustainable, and inclusive estate.

“This award demonstrates that our values are aligned, and we look forward in helping them reach their goals to drive inclusivity, reflecting and representing the diversity of the UK.”
