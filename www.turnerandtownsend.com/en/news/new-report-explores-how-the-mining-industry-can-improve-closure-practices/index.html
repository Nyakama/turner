New report explores how the mining industry can improve closure practices

28 May 2020

While also managing the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic, the mining industry continues to assess if, when and how to close end of life operations successfully and in line with shareholder, employee, union, government, regulator and community expectations.

We have joined forces with research partners Mining Journal and SRK Consulting to deliver a comprehensive study into:

  • The current industry practice for mine-closure planning and execution
  • The steps the industry needs to take to improve closure practices and ensure the optimal outcome for all stakeholders
  • Regulatory changes, and industry capacity and capability to deliver successful outcomes
  • The importance of social transitioning and sustainability

The 'Planning for successful rehabilitation' report shares the opinions of more than 400 mining industry professionals across all the major mining geographies. Download the report to read more on the challenges the mining industry faces with mine closures and where it should focus as it grapples with this important topic.
