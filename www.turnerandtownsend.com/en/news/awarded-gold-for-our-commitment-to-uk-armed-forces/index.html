Awarded Gold for our commitment to UK Armed Forces

16 July 2021

We have been recognised with a Gold award by the Ministry of Defence’s Employer Recognition Scheme for our continuous support for the armed forces community.

Our Gold award status follows signing of the Armed Forces Covenant in October 2018 and a Silver award in 2019, acknowledging our commitment to being a ‘forces friendly’ employer.

The scheme acknowledges employers who have provided exceptional support to the armed forces community and defence. The Gold award is given to a select number of organisations that go above and beyond their covenant pledges and are deemed to be exemplar within their industry.

Ed Robinson, Director, Global Defence Lead and Chair for our Military Network said:

“It is an honour and privilege to receive the Ministry of Defence’s Gold Employer Recognition Scheme award. We are better for having the wealth of skills, experience and diversity that members of the armed forces community bring to our business.”

For more information visit the Armed Forces Covenant website.
