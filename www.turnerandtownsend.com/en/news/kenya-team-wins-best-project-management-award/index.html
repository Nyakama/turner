Kenya team wins best project management award

03 May 2021

Fortifying our position in Kenya, we have won Best Project Management Business at the 2021 CoRE Awards that took place virtually on 1 May. This is our fourth CoRE Award win in three years.

The CoRE Awards bring together the construction and real estate industry to honour, network and educate through promoting best case practices in the sector. The awards scheme promotes a common industry standard, recognises emerging talent and promotes excellence for the good of the client.

Recognising and rewarding excellence across the real estate sector in Kenya, the awards rate companies across a range of attributes including sustainability, innovation and excellence.

John Rogers, Regional Director in East Africa, commented:

This award is a great recognition of our efforts in championing best practice and sustainability conversations in the built environment, and in investing in the new generation through our localisation initiatives, such as career-ready and graduate development programmes.

"Our team has been amazing throughout this year and the award is a true testament to their absolute dedication and unceasing commitment to delivering quality and upskilling talent, despite the challenges posed by the pandemic.”
