Appointed to Transport Scotland’s multiple supplier framework for maritime services

25 August 2022

We have been appointed by Transport Scotland through a framework agreement to provide maritime transportation consultancy services to support the development of a number of its maritime transport projects.

Through its multiple supplier framework agreement for maritime services, we will work with Transport Scotland to provide consultancy services across various maritime transport projects over a four-year framework.

This framework includes advise specific to maritime transportation, economic and financial services as well as maritime procurement and contract management. Alongside Ramboll and ALG, we will be leading on all of the above, plus supporting on consultation processes and procedures.

This signifies our third successful appointment with Transport Scotland, including reappointment of the Performance Audit Group (PAG), to carry out detailed audit and monitoring activities of Operations & Maintenance (O&M) contractors for the Scottish Trunk Road Network.

Steven Jackson, Director, Infrastructure, said:

“There’s already a clear vision for Scotland to create a sustainable, inclusive, safe and accessible transport system, and this appointment will allow us to further contribute to that mission.”
