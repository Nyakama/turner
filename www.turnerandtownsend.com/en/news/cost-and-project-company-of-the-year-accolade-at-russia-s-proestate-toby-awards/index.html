‘Cost and project company of the year’ accolade at Russia’s PROESTATE & TOBY awards

14 December 2021

Our Russia team has been named ‘Cost control and project management company of the year’ at the PROESTATE & TOBY awards, which took place on 9 December at the Yarovit Hall in Moscow.

The PROESTATE & TOBY awards are the only international development and real estate awards to recognise companies and projects in Russia that have demonstrated outstanding performance in their sectors.

Winning projects need to demonstrate an innovative approach, adoption of environmental, social and governance (ESG) principles, commitment to people and clients, and have made a significant contribution to the delivery of landmark projects.

The judges highlighted our participation in unique projects that bring value to the city and community, as well as adoption of ESG and zero-carbon principles. In our nomination, we highlighted our professional and independent approach and our key projects including a high-rise tower in Lakhta and a headquarters building for Shell.

Alper Tufan, Managing Director for Russia, said:

Being recognised as the 'Cost control and project management company of the year’ is a huge accolade for our team and an endorsement of the services we provide for our clients.

“Currently the Russian construction market is quite turbulent and international and local developers are looking for cost consultancy partners they can rely on. Our performance, achievements and client feedback show that our professional advice is reliable, as well as valued.

“At the same time, ESG is starting to play a significant role in the projects of large local and international developers and we are proud to be leading the field in experience and expertise.“
