Obituary: Jon White, our former UK MD and non-executive director

14 April 2020

Jon White, who has served on our Executive Board since 2010, recently passed away after a long and brave battle with cancer.

Vincent Clancy, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, said:

“Jon held a number of key roles in the business and was a major contributor over the last two decades to our success. Jon joined Turner & Townsend in 1997 within our project management business and was instrumental in the growth of the London office over the following ten years.

"Jon subsequently led our European business, developing our presence in the Netherlands, Germany, Ireland, Norway, Poland, Austria and Russia. In 2014, he became the managing director of our UK businesses growing the team to over 2,000 people and has sat on the Executive Board since 2010, more recently in a non-executive role.

Jon will be remembered for his leadership, integrity and for his passion around doing the right thing.

“Our thoughts and prayers are with his family at this time.”
