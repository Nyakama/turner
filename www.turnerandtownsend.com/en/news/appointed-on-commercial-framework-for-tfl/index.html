Appointed on commercial framework for TfL

08 June 2020

We have been appointed by Transport for London (TfL) to three lots on its commercial framework – quantity surveying, procurement, claims analysis and management.

The framework is in place to provide leading commercial expertise to TfL to support its teams in managing projects and we will be one of six businesses advising in the commercial lots. It will run for three years with an option to extend by one additional year.

To secure the appointment to the framework, we were required to identify relevant experience for each of the lots – demonstrating understanding of areas including life cycle costing, value engineering, cost management, contract and commercial management, supplier engagement and strategic procurement, claims analysis and management.

Commenting on the appointment, Richard Daley, Director in our Programme Advisory team and the client lead for TfL, said:

“We are delighted to be given the opportunity to continue our commercial support for TfL by securing these three positions on this framework. We aim to be a true value-adding partner for TfL, demonstrating a tangible return on the investment in our services and actively transferring knowledge to their teams.”

“Turner & Townsend is well placed to provide services through the framework, drawing on our extensive expertise and knowledge of capital projects across the transportation sector – both in the UK and internationally.”
