New hire to drive real estate growth in Poland

19 March 2020

We have appointed Tomasz Łosek as Director in our Warsaw office in Poland to help drive real estate growth in the region.

Tomasz joined Turner & Townsend from CBRE, bringing with him over 25 years of professional experience in our local market with particular insight into management of investment process, costs optimisation, design and facility management. During his career Tomasz has been involved in the execution of the Warsaw Trade Tower, Mokotów Busienss Center, Blue City, Wiśniowy Busienss Park and Metropolitan Building, which have become landmarks of contemporary Warsaw’s skyline. He was also vice-chair of the International Council’s steering committee for tall buildings and urban habitat.

Based in Warsaw, Tomasz’s role will focus on developer and commercial occupiers in the real estate sector, supporting growth and strengthening our project management and cost management position in the market.

Commenting on the appointment, Marcin Pawlowicz, Country Manager for Poland said:

The real estate sector in Poland continues to remain strong, particularly in Warsaw. We are seeing on-going investment activity growing in volume especially in the commercial office sector, with several spectacular office towers being delivered or planned. Therefore I’m pleased to welcome Tomasz into our team, who brings a wealth of experience in Warsaw’s market. I am certain that he will drive our continued growth in the real estate sector locally.”

Tomasz Łosek, Director said:

I am delighted to join Turner & Townsend and excited at the opportunity to strengthen our project management and cost management position, in particular among local developers, to bring added value of our service and create a new business model for the growing demands of Warsaw’s real estate sector.”
