City centre mixed-use development, Krakow

Explore

  3. City centre mixed-use development, Krakow

Providing project management and cost management services on a city centre mixed-use development in Poland.

With a courtyard and underground parking, this desirable property on Zamoyskiego Street offers 22 apartments and four commercial spaces. We were appointed to provide complex project management and cost management services throughout the investment process.

Key information

Location:
Date started:
2017
Completion date:
2018
Client type:
Real estate
Main services:
Project management, cost and commercial management

Client’s requirements

FRK BUD investment assumed execution of a seven-storey residential building with a total usable area of 2,684m2, comprising of 22 residential units and two retail units on the ground floor. The building was designed with underground car parking and equipped with a car elevator. The investment was executed within an historical area of the city, on a plot of land with complex geological conditions.

Our contribution

Applying our local knowledge and expertise in residential sector, we supported the client throughout the investment process, providing complex project management and cost management services.

Making the difference

  • Despite numerous changes being introduced, arising from some discrepancies in the design, we were able to keep additional cost to a level not exceeding two per cent of the investment value.
  • Our tailored reporting, combined with the early warning system we had established, gave the Client a constant overview of construction as it progressed, which enabled prompt and informed decision-making.

Frank Kneeshaw, CEO, FRK BUD said:

Turner & Townsend showed a proactive approach in dealing with any challenges that arose and were able to tailor solutions for every situation. Throughout the project they facilitated communication and coordination between all parties – investor, general contractors and sub-contractors. Their approach to Health & Safety was excellent.
