Making the difference

A key part of our role is to deliver improvements in managing spend and value for money. Our initiatives have delivered real benefits to SABIC, including:

  • Revised alliance recovery model - providing focus to maintenance overhead to reduce overall costs.
  • Site-wide fixed cost reporting - providing a full picture of actual and forecast costs, facilitating earlier decision making, ensuring control to budget.
  • Contract administration - rigorous governance of maintenance and project contracts on site delivers saving in excess of 50 percent of our core fee each year.
  • Implementation of a portfolio planning approach for the capital projects programme enabled SABIC to double the value of projects delivered within 12 months.
