Hudayriyat Leisure and Entertainment District, Abu Dhabi

Explore
  1. Home
  2. Projects
  3. Hudayriyat Leisure and Entertainment District, Abu Dhabi

Putting ecotourism at the forefront in Abu Dhabi.

Hudayriyat Leisure and Entertainment District is the first scheme of its kind in Abu Dhabi. The mega project’s main objective was to create a healthy and active lifestyle environment for residents and tourists while sustaining the natural environment.

Key information

Location:
Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates
Date started:
2018
Completion date:
2021
Client type:
Real estate
Main services:
Cost and commercial management, expert opinion and programme management

Leisure district

Developer Modon Properties specialises in developing vibrant and sustainable lifestyle communities. It wanted to add a scheme to the Abu Dhabi tourism portfolio with a wide range of cultural experiences and environmental features, alongside residential, entertainment, and commercial projects.

The island district boasts an impressive number of cultural and environmental attractions that are unique to its location. In addition, the unique landscape is diverse and includes Hudayriyat Leisure and Entertainment District, which is home to Marsana, Bab Al Nojoum, and Hudayriyat Heritage Trail.

As well as being home to some of the best ‘glamping’ sites in the UAE, Hudayriyat Island has numerous adventures, sports, and entertainment facilities, including a cycling track, water sports, and fitness facilities, plus a range of food and beverage outlets.

The project offers exciting sports and leisure facilities such as Circuit X, 321Sports, Bike Park, OCR Park, Hudayriyat Courts, and Hudayriyat Beach.

Complex project

The landmark development required best-in-class and innovative commercial management. Our regional expertise and comprehensive project and programme understanding ensured that we overcame any commercial challenges.

We were commissioned to deliver cost management services to ensure the project was delivered on budget. We were able to understand the complexities of the project and work successfully together with the engineer, client, and contractor to ensure robust cost control and cost management process was being implemented to deliver the project.

Kamel Elamrousy, Project Manager at GHD Consulting said:

The Turner & Townsend team has shown outstanding commitment and professionalism that contributed to the overall success of the projects.

"The flexibility they have shown, especially during the COVID-19 outbreak, was terrific and helped the team to provide continued services without compromising the quality nor program. We would definitely recommend Turner & Townsend and continue partnering with them in future projects."

Healthy lifestyle

Hudayriyat Leisure and Entertainment District officially opened to the public in November 2020. The destination points to the leadership’s vision and future development plans for Abu Dhabi, ensuring that the city remains at the forefront of global ecotourism.

It is also one of a number of projects that underline a determination to develop inclusive and unique environmental schemes and increase green spaces in a way that ensures a healthier and active lifestyle for the community, boosts social bonds, attracts visitors and tourists, and turns the emirate into a haven for sustainability.
