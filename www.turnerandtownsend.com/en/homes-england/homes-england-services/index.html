Services

We provide the following services:

  • Project management and design team services: we can deliver a full range of project management services covering the briefing development, concept design, due diligence, design and management of construction and technology requirements for new builds, refurbishments and fit-outs.
  • Programme management consultancy: we provide complete delivery using best practice programme management methodologies including programme office, risk and opportunity management, communications planning and stakeholder management.
  • Cost consultancy, procurement and commercial management: we can provide whole life cost modelling, benchmarking, budget definition, cost and commercial management and control, value management and engineering and procurement support and market testing.
  • Business case and estate strategy: we offer full support and management expertise to produce HM Treasury compliant business cases and estate strategies.
  • Masterplans: we can develop regional or site masterplans that deliver an integrated estate and local plan that integrates commercial, retail, academic and housing facilities in an effective way.
  • Construction Design and Management (CDM): we can undertake a gap analysis to identify organisational requirements to comply with CDM 2015, offering consultancy services in CDM advisor role, principal designer services and training courses.
  • Controls and performance: we apply robust and proactive controls from a clear baseline to deliver confidence in programme and project controls and performance.
