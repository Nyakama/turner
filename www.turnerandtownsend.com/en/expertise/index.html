Our expertise

Since our business began 75 years ago we have developed the skills and experience that allow us to make a valuable difference to your projects and programmes.

Sectors

Our market insight covers the full spectrum of industries, including some of the largest and most complex programmes across real estate, infrastructure and natural resources.

In every sector the market is constantly shifting, our teams stay ahead by building and sharing insight that helps to pioneer and define industry best practice.

With the best people, the right knowledge and a global reach, we maximise opportunities and minimise risks for your organisation and your projects.

Corporate and office

Finding new ways to optimise investments for both developers and occupiers of offices.

Data centres

As the global economy transforms into a digital economy, our society has never been more reliant on digital technology and resources. We are supporting data centre clients to increase capacity and manage construction risk, so they can keep delivering 24/7.

Industrial and logistics

From warehouses to gigafactories, we are helping industrial and logistics clients win business advantage and respond to fast-moving markets.

Power

Developing efficient assets to meet the energy demands of the future. Learn more about how our experts help improve manage large scale power generation projects.

Transport

We understand transport’s vital role in connecting people, businesses, cities and economies. Our insights and expertise enable clients to realise optimal programmes and projects for rail, road, sea or air.

Defence and aerospace

We are working with global clients to drive certainty in value and performance for critical and highly complex projects, programmes and portfolios in defence and aerospace.

Healthcare

We are working with healthcare clients to build and enhance major hospitals and care facilities in order to create world-class environments to improve patient outcomes.

Life sciences

For our global population of over 8 billion and rising, sustainable food, healthcare and medicines are vital. We work with the life sciences sector to enable essential research and manufacturing to meet demand.

Mining and metals

We are providing the insights and expertise to help mine owners and operators deliver programmes more cost-effectively, on time and with a focus on safe and sustainable operations.

Culture, heritage and museums

Bringing a commercial edge to privately owned, public and not-for-profit institutions.

Education

Improving the student experience is just one element of business planning for higher education projects.

Global host cities

Future host cities of major sporting and cultural events need to invest and develop wisely to ensure legacies which extend well beyond the closing ceremonies.

Hotels and hospitality

Finding the best return on investment with for hotel brands, investors and developers.

Justice and emergency services

Maintaining people’s safety and guaranteeing security is the highest priority in controlled environments.

Oil and gas

We have an extensive track record of working with energy companies globally to drive efficiency and are helping to accelerate the transition to cleaner technologies.

Residential

Helping meet spiralling housing demand and intense competition for land.

Retail

Retailers and retail led developers are constantly evolving in response to political, economic, technological and consumer changes.

Sports, leisure and lifestyle

As the sector matures, investment in projects and facilities is becoming more closely focused on profitability.

Tall buildings

Urbanisation, rising land values, the changing workplace, housing needs and sustainability pressures are all trends that are driving the proliferation of tall buildings globally.

Utilities

Delivering the efficient utilities networks required for the modern world.

Services

Investments in a specific area of a business or organisational development have an impact elsewhere. Everything is connected, so the services we offer are connected too.

Your organisation and your strategy for advancement are complex. That's why our range of expertise has the breadth and depth to cover every area of project planning, delivery and business improvement, at every stage of the project or business life cycle. The solutions we develop are based on an understanding of your overall objectives, enabling us to recognise the opportunities that will generate your greatest advantage.

Looking for a great working environment to put your skills to the right service? Explore our exciting career opportunities.

Programme strategy and set up

Project management

Controls and performance

Expert opinion

Procurement

Safety, health and quality

Digital

Productivity improvement
