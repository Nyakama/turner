Equipping road infrastructure for growth

Roads are the mainstay of our global transport networks, carrying more than 70 percent of all freight traffic. With populations growing and economies evolving, the number of vehicles on roads globally is estimated to far exceed 1 billion - and is expected to increase.

Accommodating this growth presents both a major capital cost and an opportunity. Central and local government and transportation authorities are looking to upgrade existing networks and develop new infrastructure. They are exploring advanced technological solutions, such as smart motorways, autonomous vehicles and demand management systems, to help ease traffic flow, improve the driver experience and make more efficient use of existing roads and associated transport infrastructure.

A policy push to promote a more sustainable world is increasingly focusing attention on carbon-intensive road construction and vehicles, with the roll-out of greener electric vehicles driving demand for new charging infrastructure.

Our expertise in roads

We partner with clients in the roads and highways sector to help realise the potential of projects and programmes, from complex motorways programmes to asset renewal. We understand the needs of this closely regulated environment and its funding regimes.

We apply our experience to help organisations shape their investment strategies, design the optimum organisational structure and run major capital programmes. Our knowledge of whole life costs lays the foundations for lifetime optimum performance.

We drive efficiencies and devise collaborative procurement models to help build client and supply chain capability and partner with highways agencies, local government and transport stakeholders to support on time, on budget maintenance, renewals and enhancement programmes.
