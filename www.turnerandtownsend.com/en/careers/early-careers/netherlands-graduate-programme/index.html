Graduate programme

We offer a 2-3 year graduate development programme that will provide you with both technical and behavioural training to enhance your knowledge and skills.

The programme provides you with a structured learning environment that supports you to become a well-rounded consultant and accelerate your career as far and fast as possible at one of the worldâ€™s leading construction consultancy firms.

You will be provided with a development pathway that is aligned to our internal competency frameworks as well as supporting professional development.

We currently offer the following graduate programmes:

  • Cost and commercial management
  • Project management

Opportunities are currently available in the following locations:

  • Amsterdam (Netherlands)

Due to the nature of our work you will not necessarily be based in our office locations. It is likely that you will have to visit and work from client sites over the course of your graduate programme, dependent on your role.

Who we are looking for

The applicants we recruit are strategic, analytical thinkers who see solutions where others see challenges. They come from diverse backgrounds, but usually have a strong degree, a genuine enthusiasm for how the world works, and a love for learning on the job.

Some of the qualities we look for are:

  • Strong communicator
  • Committed to making a difference
  • Quality driven
  • Exceeds expectations
  • Team player
  • Commercial thinker
  • Passion for the construction industry
  • Motivation for the scheme you have applied for (cost and commercial management, project management etc.)

Itâ€™s not just about what you studied at university we welcome applications from all degree backgrounds. We are looking for individuals who have a passion for the industry and are motivated by the work we do and the clients we work with.

Eligibility criteria:

  • Bachelors or Masters in Construction related field
  • Right to live and work in Europe
  • Fluency in English and local language
  • Clear motivation for the construction industry
  • Clear motivation for joining Turner & Townsend and the scheme you have applied for

Please check individual programme descriptions for any additional requirements. These can be found when clicking on the apply button.
