When change is the name of the game

This issue of 360°view offers a series of perspectives on how game changing businesses, projects and programmes are deploying innovative processes and technologies to enable a more sustainable and resilient world. Chief Operating Officer James Dand introduces the series.

We are going through a period of global turbulence and disruption, challenging established business norms. They have accelerated the pace of change of environmental, social and governance priorities, with geopolitical realities being reshaped and partnerships strengthened to drive value and sustainable benefits. 

The construction industry need to navigate this uncertainty, focusing on the application of science, digitilisation, data and collaboration to adapt to today's challenges and drive the global economy of the future. 

We hope that you enjoy this latest issue of 360°view.
