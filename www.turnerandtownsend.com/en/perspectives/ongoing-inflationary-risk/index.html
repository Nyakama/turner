Ongoing inflationary risk webinar

22 November 2022

The UK’s construction industry is experiencing ongoing inflationary pressure, against a turbulent economic backdrop. In our second on-demand webinar, we discuss how to mitigate the risk.

Following our recent UK market intelligence report, this latest webinar explores some important steps you can take to enable you and your business to mitigate risk at all stages of your project, as recessionary clouds gather over the economy.

Key speakers from our UK cost management, contract services, real estate and infrastructure teams hosted the webinar:

  • Managing Director of Cost Management, Martin Sudweeks
  • Director and Head of Corporate Occupier Cost Management, Oliver Sneath
  • Director and Head of Commercial Management and Supply Chain, Mike Connolly
  • Director of Contract Services - Claims & Disputes, Andrew Jeffery
  • Director of Contract Services - Procurement, Romi Alboreto
  • Associate Director, Imogen Large
  • Associate Director, Kristoffer Hudson.

Listen to discover:

  • A summary of the current economic conditions
  • The impact of these conditions on the UK construction industry including new orders, supply chain disruption, material costs, labour levels and more
  • A recap of our brilliant basics and what some clients are already doing to navigate the risks
  • The importance of keeping net zero at the forefront of agendas, despite the current economic backdrop
  • Identifying and mitigating risk for the project life cycle.
