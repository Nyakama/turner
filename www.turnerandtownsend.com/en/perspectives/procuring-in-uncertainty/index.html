Procuring in uncertainty

Romi Alboreto

Director, Contract services

UK

Email +44 (0)20 7544 4392
Romi Alboreto 17 December 2020

While Brexit remains a moving target, it is important for us all to prepare no matter what the outcome.

In the second of our on-demand webinars, in partnership with law firm Clyde & Co, we discuss the ongoing challenges posed by Brexit and the latest trends and issues facing the procurement of major projects.

In our fireside chat we considered whether all parties are ready for the transition, including taking a look at the critical pressures impacting contractors and their supply chains, advisors and clients.

Speakers included Director Romi Alboreto, Project Manager Mark Adams and Clyde & Co Partner David Hansom.

Listen to discover:

  • The need for an in-depth approach to supply chain management
  • Integrating programme, cost and risk forecasting
  • Impacts associated with existing contracts
  • Views on procurement options after Brexit.
