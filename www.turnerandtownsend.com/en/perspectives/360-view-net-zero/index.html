The equilibrium of net zero

This issue of 360°view explores the net-zero theme, offering a series of perspectives on how we can lead with sustainable solutions and futureproof projects and programmes across the real estate, infrastructure and natural resources sectors. Chief Operating Officer James Dand introduces the series.

We are at a critical point in the race to tackle the climate crisis, with precious ecosystems receding and people’s health and livelihoods under threat.

The construction industry has an important role to play in dramatically reducing global greenhouse gas emission levels, using the most innovative technologies. At the same time, emissions must be removed from the atmosphere using both nature-based and technological removal approaches. By bringing emissions back into balance with those captured, we can achieve the equilibrium of net zero.

We hope that you enjoy this latest issue of 360°view.
