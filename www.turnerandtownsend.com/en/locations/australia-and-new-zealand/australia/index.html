Turner & Townsend in Australia

Supporting the full life cycle on some of Australia’s biggest projects.

Australia is experiencing moderating economic growth following the resources boom, with weak exchange rates boosting tourism, education and exports. The residential sector in Australia is strong for now, while infrastructure spending is unequally distributed across the states.

Making the difference in Australia

In Australia, we are renowned for providing cost management, project management and program management services to clients in the real estate, infrastructure and natural resources sectors, consulting for all levels of governments and private enterprise. Our service offering extends to provide an integrated solution for every stage of an asset’s life cycle, across consultancy, delivery, operations, from advisory to delivery and operations, as well as portfolio and program management.

We are a market leader in Australia, with one of the largest specialised independent service offerings in the region, with a diverse team of professionals from supporting backgrounds and with a deep pool of subject matter experts who work closely with all project teams. 

Our high-profile infrastructure projects include Snowy Hydro, Inland Rail and Sydney Metro Northwest. In the real estate space, we are supporting the new Sydney Fish Market, The Jewel and EastCo development, and in natural resources, our major projects include Rum Jungle mine rehabilitation, Tellus Holdings' Sandy Ridge Kaolin Mine and Woodside Energy Scarborough. 

In 2022, we acquired JukesTodd, an advisory and project management firm based in Australia. Doubling the size of our natural resources business in Australia, our combined team works together as Turner & Townsend JukesTodd offering project and programme management, commercial management and advisory including environmental, social and governance (ESG) services across the mining and clean energy sectors.
