Turner & Townsend in Germany

Select a language:

  • Deutsch

Helping our clients realise their full potential.

We are currently experiencing a volatile and highly-diverse economic environment. Nevertheless, demand is strong in the commercial and public construction sector. It is therefore all the more important that construction projects and programmes are robustly defined, aligned and structured in terms of its project objectives, and then consistently implemented and managed with these objectives in mind.

Making the difference in Germany

We have more than 20 years of experience and local market expertise in our German office, and we recognise that there’s no ‘one size fits all’ template. Understanding our clients’ priorities and needs is important to us in order to be an agile and reliable partner when delivering impactful capital programmes and projects in social and transport infrastructure, process industries and renewables, corporate and office, data centres, industrial and logistics, life sciences, retail, and hotels and hospitality.

With a team of over 300 experts working from our offices in Berlin, Hamburg, Frankfurt, Stuttgart, Braunschweig and Munich, we support our local and international clients from the private and public sectors.

From project management and project controls to cost and commercial management, we develop the best solutions for our clients in Germany. We are a diverse and inclusive business that reflects the society in which we live, and we care about our community and building a sustainable future.

We create solutions that take our clients’ projects forward by addressing the challenge of a constantly changing world. Working with the best people from all over the world, we are able to deliver innovative services for our clients.

Our not-for-profit Turner & Townsend Foundation in Germany was set up to promote research, teaching and training for our project and cost managers of the future.

For more information, please contact Dr Stefan Reimoser, Managing Director, Germany and Austria.
