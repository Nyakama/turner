Turner & Townsend in Spain

After a debilitating crisis, the construction sector is on its way to recovery. We’re supporting clients to play their part in shaping this new era of construction and development.

Despite political uncertainty, which is affecting investor confidence, there is steady overall growth in the Spanish construction industry. The residential real estate sector is currently enjoying increased foreign investment, and house prices are set to rise steadily. At the same time, the government is re-energising the infrastructure sector with programmes of rail and road modernisation.

Making the difference in Spain

Our presence in Spain allows us to bring local and international expertise to global clients operating in the region. Our work includes projects that incorporate significant complexity, international stakeholders and cultural challenges. Recently we have delivered programme management for the deployment of new retail concepts for automotive brands and project and cost management for office fit-outs, in both modern and traditional buildings.
