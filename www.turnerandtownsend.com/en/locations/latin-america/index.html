Turner & Townsend in Latin America

Turner & Townsend has been supporting both local and global clients in Latin America since 2000. We provide an essential combination of local knowledge and expertise with global best practice in programme, project and cost management, making the difference in successful programme and project delivery for many of the worlds leading companies. We operate across our three global sectors of natural resources, infrastructure and real estate, specialising in oil and gas, power, mining and metals, commercial, retail, pharmaceutical, hi-tech and manufacturing.
