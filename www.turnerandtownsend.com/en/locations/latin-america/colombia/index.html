Turner & Townsend in Colombia

The construction and property sector is going from strength to strength, with diverse infrastructure and real estate opportunities attracting increased foreign investment. We’re helping clients set their projects up for success against this exciting backdrop.

There have been significant changes to Colombia’s urban landscape in recent years, with the growing middle class driving demand for residential construction. Now, challenges from the lack of space to build in cities is prompting more and more vertical construction and limiting the pace and potential of projects.

With the mining and energy sectors facing a slowdown, transport infrastructure is set to play an increasingly important role in generating income for the country, and multiple large-scale projects on roads and highways are currently underway.

Making the difference in Colombia

Our office is located in the capital Bogota, where our team of experts work across our three operating sectors to support clients on a diverse spread of projects. We specialise in driving certainty and transparency in cost and schedule for capital projects and drive best practice standards in setting up projects for success.
