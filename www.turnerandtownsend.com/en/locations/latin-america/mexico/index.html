Turner & Townsend in Mexico

Delivering specialist cross-sector expertise in a buoyant economic landscape.

Mexico’s strong and buoyant economy combined with their open market policies provide excellent opportunities for both local enterprises and global investors entering the country.

Major investment is forecast in the oil and gas industry with new licences being auctioned this year, the mining industry continues in a stable state despite the global mining downturn. Major infrastructure programmes are underway and real estate investment is increasing with manufacturing being particularly strong. We look forward to continuing supporting our major global and local clients in this buoyant marketplace.

Making the difference in Mexico

We started working in Mexico in 2000 with our first PPP project on a major new hospital. Since then we have supported our global clients with their investments into Mexico, most notably Nissan where we have been undertaking project and cost management services on their Latin American rollout of new showrooms across the region.

Our offer of programme, project and cost management is delivered by our Mexican nationals supported with key sector specialists with experience in the natural resources, infrastructure and real estate sectors.
