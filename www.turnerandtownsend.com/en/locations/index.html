Our offices

With offices located globally, you're never far away from our services. Working from 118 offices in 50 countries, we make the difference to projects across the real estate, infrastructure and natural resources sectors worldwide.

Our consultants are happy to discuss your requirements over the phone, via email or in person, depending on what suits you.

If you would like to speak to someone at one of our country offices, you can find these details by selecting from the list below. Alternatively, contact us directly if your enquiry relates to a sector or service.

Interested in a a career at Turner & Townsend? Find out more about our current opportunities and apply directly online.

If you want to email us, we will find the most suitable person to respond to your query and they will be in touch shortly.
