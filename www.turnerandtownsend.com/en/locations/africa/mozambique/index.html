Turner & Townsend in Mozambique

Helping clients get the best return on their investment in one of sub-Saharan Africa's main construction hubs

Mozambique is home to some of the world's largest gas and coal reserves and holds great potential for development. Improvements in infrastructure are helping to accelerate this growth. 

The country is currently heavily reliant on foreign investment in large-scale projects to build and repair roads, expand harbours and rehabilitate railways.

Mozambique’s strategy to optimise infrastructure investments is to focus on key growth areas. The Maputo Development Corridor is considered one of the most well-developed and successful corridors in sub-Saharan Africa in generating local economic development.

Making the difference in Mozambique

We have more than a decade of experience working in Mozambique on mining, oil and gas, infrastructure and real estate related projects. Our local staff speak Portuguese and have a deep understanding of the specialised disciplines required to deliver large, complex projects. We proactively support local contractors to deliver our clients’ requirements within international best practice standards.
