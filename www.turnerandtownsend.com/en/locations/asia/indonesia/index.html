Turner & Townsend in Indonesia

While the construction sector is thriving overall, the oil and gas sectors are facing setbacks. We’re supporting clients to get the most out of projects, whatever the unique industry context.

The palm oil industry is one of Indonesia's key foreign exchange earners and it is the world's largest producer and exporter of palm oil. The Indonesian construction industry is currently benefitting from significant foreign investment and international partnerships, which have led to large improvements in the sector’s capacity, expertise and overall capability. At the same time, the oil and gas sectors – vital contributors to the country’s economy – are facing challenges from increased production costs and lack of adequate technology.

Making the difference in Indonesia

We have had a presence in Indonesia since 2010. We provide project services and project controls (schedule, cost estimating, commercial contracts and risk for projects) to some of the largest organisations in Asia across the power, hi-tech and manufacturing, retail and oil and gas sectors.
