Turner & Townsend in South Korea

We’re helping clients achieve their business goals in a mature, competitive construction market

South Korea is in a strong position within the overseas Asian market, with firms consistently winning lucrative contracts and projects benefiting from advanced construction technology.

In South Korea, rising numbers of tourists and a growing population are encouraging activity in the commercial real estate sectors. At the same time, the government’s commitment to achieving zero-energy housing and reducing carbon emissions is driving demand for sustainable and green construction techniques.

Making the difference in South Korea

Turner & Townsend Korea was established as a joint venture by 50 percent investment respectively with Hanmi Global, a leading Korean company in construction management which has sold cost management services since 1996, covering over 1000 projects in South Korea and abroad. We were founded to provide systematic and professional cost management services in the South Korean construction market.

In South Korea we deliver services including cost management, project management, risk, contract, procurement, claim, and life cycle costing.
