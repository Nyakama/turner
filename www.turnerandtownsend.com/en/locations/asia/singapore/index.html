Turner & Townsend in Singapore

Helping our clients achieve great outcomes on some of Singapore’s largest projects and programmes.

Turner & Townsend celebrates 75 years of service excellence, setting new standards and transforming performance across the industry. We make the difference for our clients and communities by building trust to deliver green and inclusive projects and programmes globally.

We were established in Singapore in 2000, a key strategic location with its friendly and vibrant business environment, political stability and economic strength. It is the regional headquarters for South East Asia and home to many of the world’s largest organisations in the real estate, infrastructure and natural resources sectors.

For more than 20 years, we have delivered over 600 projects, supporting key government initiatives, a regional data centre hub, a market leader for education and a research and development centre for industrial and logistics. We have worked with some of the world’s biggest clients in the corporate and office, residential and developer and public sectors.

We have helped shape and improve Singapore’s built environment, creating better places for people to live, work and play.

Making the difference in Singapore

Our Singapore business is PM01 and QS01, registered by the Building and Construction Authority (BCA) of Singapore. Our business management systems are accredited for Quality (ISO 9001), Safety (ISO 45001) and Environmental (ISO 14001).

We have over 100 professional staff working in Singapore. Our Managing Director Khoo Sze Boon was awarded RICS Construction Professional of the Year 2020 and leads in the development and expansion of the business across all services. Together, we build a positive reputation and capability based on our programme management approach from initial advisory services through to project management delivery, cost management, project controls, procurement, contract services, portfolio and programme management and operational consultancy.
