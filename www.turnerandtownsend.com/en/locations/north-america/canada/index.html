Project monitoring

We carry out detailed reviews of development projects, offering independent and impartial advice on project risks. We work closely with all project stakeholders and offer a solution led approach. Our thorough review covers the development budget, schedule, procurement and contracts and financing documentation. Our service is delivered in a comprehensive, concise report highlighting risks that may impact the project.

Our project monitoring service is tailored to suit the needs of our clients and is designed to provide peace of mind that the development risks are being well managed and to assure that the budget and cost to complete is adequate. We are passionate about maintaining great communication and developing long-lasting relationships and believe the best way to keep our clients up to date is to speak regularly and or meet in person.

At the centre/heart of everything we do is our service quality and professional integrity and every project is overseen by our senior management and delivered in conjunction with the RICS/CIQS joint guidance note; project monitoring for real estate lending, Canada, first edition.
